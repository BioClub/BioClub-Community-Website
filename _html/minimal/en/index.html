BioHack Academy 2022

The BioHack Academy is an international, 3-month programme to learn the basics of biology, biotechnoly, how to use a lab, how to make open science hardware, and how to explore the social and ethical issues surrounding the emerging biotechnolgies.

The programme is a collaboration with the Waag Society in Amsterdam, NL. More infor about the global BioHack Academy.
